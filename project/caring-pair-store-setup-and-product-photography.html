Client

Caring Pair

About Client

Caring Pair helps to send hampers to your loved ones to show them that you care. They offer four different types of care packages: classic package, chocolate-galore, self-care package and coffree O’ clock.

The Challenge

Being a store with small amount of items to sell, they would not have a lot of information to share, thus it would be quite a difficulty to fill up information on the website especially the homepage.

The store also helps send personalised gifts to loved ones. Thus, the customer would need to be able to send their own personalised message and picture.

The Solution

I focused mostly on the visual presentation to fill up most of the content on the home page. I added more visually striking images and animation that fills up most of the pages.

I also added a promotion section which announces to the customers for any promotion that is going on creating an incentive for the customer to buy their products.

I also integrated an app called “Infinite Product” which provides a form in the product page for customers to send their own personalised messages and images to their loved ones.

The Result

By focusing on the visual presentation and a more personalised product, it helps to improve the branding and value of company’s products.
